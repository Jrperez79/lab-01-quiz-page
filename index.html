Howdy Ya'll, My Name Is Willie! Learn About Me Below...

Here is some Info about me....

I was born in Abbott, Texas, on April 29, 1933. I played football, basketball, and baseball in high school. I started singing in my teens. My first band in high school played a morning spot at a local station. I was in the Air Force for nine months before returning and going to Baylor University. I moved to the Portland metro area to live with my mom a couple years after college. I worked on my music and moved back to Austin, Texas in the early 70's and my career took off. I have been lucky to have a awesome career and work some great artists!

Famous Artists I have played with...

  • Johnny Cash
  • Waylon Jennings
  • Kris Kristofferson
